Registration Info

From academic year 2019-2020 onwards, all projects associated with external organisations are administratively managed through an online registration procedure. You start the registration via SisA (Requests-Mobility-Request Mobility/Internship) and complete the process in Mobility Online. In Mobility Online, you follow a step-by-step plan for the complete administration. Complete all steps at least 3 weeks before the start of your internship. Only after final approval are you insured as an intern.
